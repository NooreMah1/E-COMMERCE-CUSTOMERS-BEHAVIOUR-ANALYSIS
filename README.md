# E-COMMERCE-CUSTOMERS-BEHAVIOUR-ANALYSIS
Customer Behavior Analysis using Python to evaluate spending patterns, satisfaction levels, discount effectiveness, and retention insights for data-driven business decisions
📊 Customer Behavior Analysis & Retention Strategy

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce customer data to understand purchasing behavior, satisfaction levels, and retention patterns. The goal is to derive actionable insights that can help businesses improve customer engagement and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Objective
Analyze customer spending patterns
Evaluate the impact of discounts on behavior
Understand factors influencing customer satisfaction
Identify retention risks using purchase recency
Develop insights for targeted business strategies
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Python
Pandas
NumPy
Seaborn
Matplotlib
Jupyter Notebook
📂 Dataset Features

The dataset includes:

Customer demographics (Age, Gender, City)
Membership Type
Total Spend & Items Purchased
Average Rating & Satisfaction Level
Discount Applied (True/False)
Days Since Last Purchase
🔍 Analysis Performed
1. Customer Segmentation

Customers were grouped based on city, membership type, and satisfaction level to identify high-value segments.

2. Satisfaction Analysis

Explored how ratings and discounts relate to customer satisfaction levels.

3. Promotion Strategy

Analyzed whether discounts influence:

Spending behavior
Purchase quantity
Customer ratings
4. Retention Analysis

Used Days Since Last Purchase to identify inactive customers and potential churn risk.

5. Multivariate Analysis

Used visualizations like pairplots and heatmaps to study relationships between:

Spending
Purchasing behavior
Satisfaction
Customer activity
📊 Key Insights
Discounts do not significantly influence customer satisfaction or spending behavior
Customer inactivity (higher days since last purchase) is strongly linked to:
Lower spending
Reduced engagement
Customers who purchase more items tend to spend more and give better ratings
Significant variation exists across cities, indicating location-based behavior differences
💡 Business Recommendations
Avoid over-reliance on discounts as a primary strategy
Focus on improving customer experience and engagement
Target inactive customers with personalized retention strategies
Prioritize high-value customer segments based on behavior and location
📈 Visualizations

The project includes:

Pairplots for multivariate analysis
Heatmaps for correlation analysis
Bar charts for segmentation insights
